Colorize is an image processing web app. It allows users to upload their images and run them through a deep learning neural network that can colorize, enhance and toonify the images.


The web app supports

  • Colorizing black and white photos
  • Enhance the quality and resolution of low quality images.
  • Toonify images

How does it work? Magic! - Just kidding! It based on deep learning technology that uses a neural network to train and to learn from thousands of photos and their colored counterparts and ability to fill and enhance the image. Once the models have been fed many photos it learns to color and enhance photos.
